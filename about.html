Who We Are

At SBB Foundation, we believe that true success is measured not by profits, but by the positive change we create. We are a unique blend of a software powerhouse and a purpose-driven non-profit.

On one side, our team builds modern, scalable, and reliable software solutions that help businesses and NGOs thrive in the digital age. On the other, we channel those very earnings into transformative social initiatives — providing education to underprivileged children, empowering rural communities, and nurturing sustainability through our cow ranch projects.

Our Impact

Every rupee we earn from software services goes directly into social good.

  • 📘 Free education programs for underprivileged children.
  • 🐄 A sustainable cow ranch supporting rural livelihoods.
  • 🌍 Community development initiatives for dignity and opportunity.
