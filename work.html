Our Programs

Programs that change lives

We run practical, community-rooted programs in education and animal welfare to create sustainable impact.

Child Education

We run after-school classes, distribute books & uniforms, provide scholarships and hold creative learning sessions to build confidence and skills.

  • After-school tuition & mentoring
  • Monthly learning kits & stationery
  • Scholarships for meritorious students

Cow Ranch & Animal Welfare

Our ranch cares for rescued and sheltered cows, provides veterinary care and produces organic manure used in sustainable farming initiatives.

  • Health camps & vaccination drives
  • Feeding & shelter programs
  • Organic manure & small-scale farm training

Community Engagement

Workshops, youth-skills training, and local enterprise support to increase employment and empower families.
