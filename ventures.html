Other Ventures by Our Founder

Alongside the SBB Foundation, our founder has built strong businesses to serve the financial needs of the community.

SBB Finance – Empowering Lives Through Financial Support

Founded by Jitendra Sharma, SBB Finance is a socially-driven financial service that aims to uplift underprivileged communities by offering small, accessible loans to those in need. With a deep understanding of grassroots challenges, we are committed to helping individuals start small businesses, manage emergencies, or support their families with dignity and independence. Our goal is not just to provide money, but to build trust, create opportunities, and foster financial independence for people who are often overlooked by traditional banks. Through transparent practices and personalized support, SBB Finance stands as a beacon of hope for many striving for a better future. “We believe that a small loan at the right time can change a life. That’s why SBB Finance exists — to be that helping hand.”

— Jitendra Sharma, Founder.
