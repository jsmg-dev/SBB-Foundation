Nurturing Cows with Care at Our Ranch

At SBB Foundation, we believe that every life deserves compassion. Our cow ranch is a peaceful space where cows are nurtured with love, proper care, and dignity. We provide them with nutritious food, clean shelter, and regular medical attention. Many of our cows are rescued or abandoned, and through our efforts, we give them a second chance at life. This initiative reflects our commitment to animal welfare and our broader mission of kindness and care for all living beings.

Empowering Poor Children Through Education

Education is the most powerful tool to break the cycle of poverty. SBB Foundation is committed to providing underprivileged children with access to quality learning, a safe environment, and the resources they need to grow and succeed. Through school support, tuition programs, and educational activities, we help children build confidence, gain knowledge, and dream of a better future.
